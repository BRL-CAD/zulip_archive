Oh yes, this. I also had this problem. You'll have to assign a new material to the sphere instance. After this, the screen will probably turn black though, which is because the cameras is too "into" the screen. You will have to move the camera "away from the screen" by doing I believe Ctrl-Right click which will dolly the camera.
