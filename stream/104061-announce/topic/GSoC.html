Sean (Jan 13 2025 at 18:15):

There are lots of resources to find issues once you've obtained the code and got a compilation working. @Divy singhvi @Vedan Yadav

You can look at the BUGS and TODO text files in the checkout which have hundreds of items of varying complexity. There's also https://brlcad.org/~sean/ideas.html which has lots of topics (some are out of date or in-progress, so discuss to discover). About half of the topics at https://brlcad.org/wiki/Google_Summer_of_Code/Project_Ideas are valid, half are not (so again, discuss to discover which). Lastly there are last year's GSoC list at https://github.com/opencax/GSoC/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3A%22GSoC+2024%22+label%3A%22Project%3A+BRL-CAD%22 which hasn't been updated yet but has topics worth discussing.
