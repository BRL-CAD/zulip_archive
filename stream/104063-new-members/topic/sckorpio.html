Daniel Rossberg (Dec 03 2018 at 18:54):

The translate etc. will be applied to one of the matrices which are part of the combination data structure. That's why they cannot be applied to any object directly. What you could do is create a new combination, make the main group part of it, do the transformation, xpush it, and kill the new combination.
