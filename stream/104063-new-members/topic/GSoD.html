Sean (May 31 2019 at 13:13):

@Chetan Shinde Congratulations on your report. It looks nice. I like the integrated diagrams and overall layout.

If you're looking for suggestions on making it better, I would offer just a couple points of advice -- one would be to follow a particular style guide, which you may have been doing already. That usually helps with consistency and norms for readers. For example, using Latin numerals is usually reserved for preambles in books and reports, not for the main body. Not a big deal, but it starts to get important when document pages get into the dozens or hundreds of pages. Another point would be to have the grammar checked by a second set of eyes. I noticed a couple phrasing issues in the document, none major but they were enough to distract from the content in places. Otherwise, I think the use of LaTeX is perfect for this kind of report and that's exactly what it was made for. The layout looks really nice.

Sean (May 31 2019 at 13:16):
